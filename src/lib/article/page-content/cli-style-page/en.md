## What is an ASCII Text Generator?

An **ASCII text generator** is a tool that converts normal text characters into larger, decorative text made up of ASCII characters. This form of digital art, known as ASCII art, dates back to the early days of computing.

**ASCII art** is a graphic design technique that uses computers for presentation and consists of pictures pieced together from the 95 printable characters defined by the ASCII Standard. Historically, it was used when early printers lacked graphics ability. Today, it's a popular way to add style to plain text environments.
