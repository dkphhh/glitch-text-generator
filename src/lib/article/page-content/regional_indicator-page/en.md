## Character Mapping Technology

The regional indicator generator maps standard English letters to corresponding Unicode regional indicators. Since regional indicators only include uppercase letters A-Z:

- Lowercase letters are converted to uppercase
- Numbers and symbols typically remain unchanged or are filtered
- Spaces and punctuation may require special handling

Regional indicators have some important usage limitations:

- **Character limits**: Due to large display size, not suitable for long text
- **Platform dependent**: Display effect highly depends on platform and device
- **Performance considerations**: Excessive use may affect page performance
