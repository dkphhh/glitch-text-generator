## What is an ASCII Text Generator?

An **ASCII text generator** (or **ASCII banner generator**) is a tool that converts normal text into large, decorative text made up of ASCII characters. These "banners" use standard keyboard characters—like `#`, `/`, `\`, and `_`—to form larger letters and shapes.

Historically, **ASCII art** is a graphic design technique that dates back to the early days of computing (around 1966!). It was invented because early printers lacked graphics capabilities. Today, it's a beloved retro aesthetic used everywhere from hacker forums to modern chat apps.
