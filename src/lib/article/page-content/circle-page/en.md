The circled text generator creates a unique decorative effect where each character is wrapped in a circle. This effect is achieved through enclosed alphanumeric symbols in Unicode.

## Enclosed Characters in Unicode

The Unicode standard includes a dedicated character block called "Enclosed Alphanumerics." This block contains various characters surrounded by geometric shapes, with circled characters being the most common.

The original design purposes of these characters include:

- Numbering and list markers
- Step indicators
- Emphasis and decoration
- Special typographic needs

## Visual Characteristics of Circled Characters

Circled characters have distinctive visual features:

- Each character is surrounded by a complete circle
- Circles are typically white background with black border or transparent background
- Characters are centered within the circle
- Overall presents a neat and unified visual effect

## Character Limitations

- Numbers 0-9
- Uppercase letters A-Z

Not all characters have corresponding circled versions. For characters without corresponding versions, the generator typically keeps them as is or uses alternative solutions.
