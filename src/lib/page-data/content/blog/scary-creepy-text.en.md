## Psychological Effects of Scary Text

Why do these distorted texts make people uneasy? There are deep psychological principles behind this:

### 1. Violates Cognitive Expectations

The human brain is accustomed to processing orderly, organized information. When text shows abnormal distortion and chaos, the brain generates an alert response, triggering feelings of insecurity.

### 2. Uncanny Valley Effect

Scary text falls between "readable" and "unreadable," this ambiguous state activates the brain's uncanny valley response, producing eeriness.

### 3. Supernatural Associations

Chaotic text makes people think of:

- Ancient cult runes
- Cursed magic books
- Messages left by evil spirits
- Digital world collapse
- Cracks and glitches in reality

### 4. Visual Impact

Dense special symbols and irregular character arrangements bring strong stimulus to the visual system, enhancing the horror atmosphere.
